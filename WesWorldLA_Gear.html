Nothing beats my iPhone. It’s always on me. It records 4k 60fps effortlessly (though I never post 4k). It’s easy to access if inspiration hits you anywhere you are.

I love the DJI Osmo Pocket. It’s a 3 axis gimbal that fits in your pocket for smooth cinema-like videos. It’s great for vlogging because the microphone faces you and the camera tracks your face so you are always in frame. The rear touch screen also lets you know that you’ve got the perfect shot.

DJI’s Osmo Action is quickly becoming a favorite. The microphone isn’t the best but its a tough little camera that can take a beating. It also has a feature called Rocksteady that eliminates practically all shakes and unwanted movements, even when running. The front facing screen is also a huge plus for creating YouTube content.

The DJI Spark is a powerful entry level drone. It shoots 1080p 60fps with a 2-axis gimbal for super steady shots. You can easily achieve beautiful cinematic shots with a little practice. Go for the Fly-More combo with RC. You (can) fly this with just your phone but…don’t. The experience is pretty bad as is the range. The RC really opens the capabilities of what this little drone can do. If you do have the extra money go for the DJI Mavic Pro. That's next on my drone wish list and I’ll talk about it more whenever I get it.
